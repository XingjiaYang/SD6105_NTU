Source & Logic
ACADEMIC Normalized based on SAT Math 75th Percentile scores. Average of reported years.
SELECTIVITY Calculated as (1 - Admission Rate). Higher score indicates a more competitive/selective process.
GRADUATION Based on the total graduation rate (typically 150% normal time). Average of 2017-2023.
AFFORDABILITY Based on In-state Tuition & Fees. Calculated as (1 - Normalized Cost). Higher score means "More Affordable".
FINANCIAL AID The average percentage of full-time first-time undergraduates receiving any financial aid.
